How to Play

A quick guide to get you started on Play or Pay.

  • 1

    15 Questions

    The game consists of 15 questions. One player asks, and the other responds.

  • 2

    Vibe & Chat

    If you're not feeling the questions, you can 'vibe' and chat casually instead.

  • 3

    Pay the Price

    If a question is unsatisfactory or unanswered, the player must transfer 50 tokens to their opponent.
